About FeelTune

Your personalized music journey, powered by your mood.

What is FeelTune?

FeelTune is a cutting-edge mood-based playlist generator that analyzes your emotions and curates the perfect soundtrack for your day. Using advanced integrations with music APIs like Spotify, we bring you playlists that resonate with how you feel.

Key Features

  • Mood Detection: Input your mood (e.g., "happy," "relaxed") or analyze your feelings through text.
  • Playlist Generation: Instantly fetch mood-specific playlists or songs with direct links to play them.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Save your favorite playlists, explore suggestions based on your listening history, and revisit your most loved tunes anytime.
  • Interactive Interface: Enjoy a clean and modern design with mood icons and an intuitive user experience.

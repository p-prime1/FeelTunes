Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [12/2024]

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and username during registration. Mood preferences and playlists you create or interact with.
  • Usage Data: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address. Interaction data such as buttons clicked and pages visited.
  • Optional Data: Feedback or messages sent through support or contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of FeelTune.
  • Personalize your experience by recommending mood-based playlists.
  • Improve our services based on user feedback and behavior.
  • Communicate updates, features, or changes to the app.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you provide explicit consent to share information with third-party services.
  • For Legal Obligations: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, or API integration.

4. Third-Party Services

FeelTune integrates with third-party services like Spotify and Last.fm for playlist generation. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@feeltune.com.
